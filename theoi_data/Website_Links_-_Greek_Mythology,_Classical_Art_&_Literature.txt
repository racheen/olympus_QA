    <h2>
     MYTHOLOGY WEBSITES
    </h2>
    <p>
     <a href="http://www.maicar.com/GML/" target="_blank">
      Greek Mythology Link
      <br/>
     </a>
     A collection of the Greek myths written by Carlos Parada, author of the book Genealogical Guide to Greek Mythology (published in 1993) based on original sources, that is, authors from the period between 800 BC and AD 600. The site contains texts, images, maps and charts.
    </p>
    <p>
     <a href="http://www.mythindex.com/" target="_blank">
      Myth Index
      <br/>
     </a>
     More than 3,700 names from Greek mythology. A comprehensive dictionary of Greek mythology, including titles and epithets of the gods.
    </p>
    <p>
     <a href="http://www.timelessmyths.com/" target="_blank">
      Timeless Myths
     </a>
     <br/>
     A collection of myths and legend from Classical Greece and Rome, Norse and Celtic mythology, as well as Arthurian legend.
    </p>
    <p>
     <a href="http://www.mythweb.com/" target="_blank">
      Mythweb
     </a>
     <br/>
     A site is devoted to the heroes, gods and monsters of Greek mythology.
    </p>
    <p>
     <a href="http://www.livingmyths.com/" target="_blank">
      Living Myths
     </a>
     <br/>
     Mythology: Greek, Celtic, Native American and Chinese myths retold and interpreted.
    </p>
    <p>
     <a href="http://www.paleothea.com/" target="_blank">
      Women in Greek Myths
     </a>
     <br/>
     A who's-who on all females in Greek mythology, with a section on Greek Men, a collection of myths, and many beautiful images.
    </p>
    <p>
     <a href="http://www.mythman.com/" target="_blank">
